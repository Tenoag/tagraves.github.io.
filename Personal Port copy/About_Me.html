About Me

My name is Tenoa, and I am a current student at Wake Tech studying Cyber Security.

My passion is within the technology field and IT. It has its ups and downs as technology rapidly changes, making it hard to find where you fit in, but I make it work.

Originally, I was born in Mississippi, but sadly my mother and I got caught in the middle of Hurricane Katrina, which took a lot of what we had. So since we no longer have a home, we moved up here to North Carolina, where I'd eventually be staying. As I grew up, a lot of what my parents gave me to entertain myself were tablets or phones because I didn't have much interest in toys. However, my interest in technology didn't really spark until over break in middle school. There was a group I got signed up for which involved coding a program for the Sphero balls to go through an obstacle course to present to big businesses/corporations.

While I participated in that, I had a lot of fun, and I was able to quickly understand how the programming for the Spheros worked. Ever since then, the idea of coding or designing something stuck with me. I eventually took coding classes in high school and tried to learn extra when I had free time at home, but I couldn't stay focused for long when I did.
